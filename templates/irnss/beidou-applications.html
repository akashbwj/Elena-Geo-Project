Applications of BeiDou

  • Transportation

    Transportation is the lifeblood of national economy, social development and people’s life. The BeiDou Satellite Navigation System is an important channel to help in realizing the in formalization and modernization of transportation. BDS is of great significance to the establishment of a modern transportation system that is smooth, efficient, safe and green. The sector mainly includes land applications, navigation applications, and aviation applications. Land applications include vehicle autonomous navigation, vehicle tracking and monitoring, vehicle intelligent information system, networking applications, railway operation monitoring, and etc. Navigation applications include ocean transportation, inland river navigation, ship anchorage and docking, and etc. Aviation applications include route navigation, airport scene monitoring, precision, and etc. With the development of the transportation industry, the demands of high-precision applications are increasing rapidly.

  • Agriculture

    China is a large agricultural country. In combination with remote sensing, geographic information, and other technologies, BDS technologies have accelerated the transformation from traditional agriculture to intelligent agriculture. In that way, the cost of agricultural production could be significantly reduced, and the agricultural labor productivity and labor income would be increased. The sector mainly includes the applications of farmland information collection, soil nutrient and distribution survey, crop fertilization, crop disease and pest prevention and control, planting monitoring, agricultural machinery unmanned driving, farmland ridging sowing, UAV plant protection, and etc. The applications like agricultural machinery unmanned driving, farmland ridging sowing, UAV plant protection have a strong demand for the high-precision BDS services.

  • Forestry

    Forestry is one of the earlier BDS applications. Forestry management departments use BDS to carry out forestry resource inventory, forestry management and inspection. In that way, the management cost has been greatly reduced and the work efficiency of forestry has been well improved. The sector mainly includes the applications of forest area estimation, timber volume estimation, ranger woodland patrol, forest-fire prevention, determination of regional boundaries, and etc. Among them, ranger woodland patrol and forest-fire prevention utilizes the unique BDS SMS functions. In particular, combined with remote sensing technologies, BDS has played an important role in the National Forest Resources Survey. Moreover, with the implementation of collective forest ownership in China’s forest areas, BDS has also been widely applied in boundaries exploration and ownership recognition.

  • Fishery

    Fishery is an industry which the BDS SMS services were popularized earlier and widely used. China is a large fishery country, the area of Chinese marine fishery covers about 3,000,000 km2, and there are a great number of fishing vessels and Chinese fishermen engaged in marine fishery. The sector mainly includes the applications of fishing vessel navigation, fishery administration, fishing port management, marine disaster warning, short message communication for fishermen, and etc. Especially in the sea areas where there are no mobile communication signals, using the BDS short message function, the fisherman can send messages to their families through BDS terminal equipment. In this way, fishermen’s safety, national marine economic security, marine resources protection and maritime sovereignty maintenance can all be effectively protected.

  • Public Security

    A large number of public security services such as anti-terrorism, social stability maintenance, social security demand for high sensitivity and confidentiality. Therefore, it is imperative to popularize BDS in the field of public security. A BDS-based public security information system has realized the dynamic dispatch and integrated command of police resources, which has improved the response time and the execution efficiency. The sector mainly includes the command and dispatch of public security vehicles, the on-site law enforcement management, emergency incident information transmission, public security time service, and other applications. Among them, the emergency incident information transmissions can be implemented by using the unique BDS short message function.

  • Disaster Prevention and Mitigation

    The field of disaster prevention and mitigation is one of the most prominent BDS industrial applications. Through the short message and location reporting function of BDS, disaster warning and quick report, disaster relief command and dispatch, and rapid emergency communication have been realized. This can greatly improve the response time and decision-making capability of the disaster emergency response. The sector mainly includes the applications of disaster reporting, disaster warning, disaster relief command, disaster communication, buildings, bridges and reservoirs monitoring, and etc. Among them, disaster relief command and disaster communication can use the unique BDS short message function, while the buildings, bridges and reservoirs monitoring applications could use the high-precision BDS services.

  • Special Care

    In recent years, BeiDou applications for special group care has been gradually emerging. Through the BDS navigation, positioning, short message and other functions, the relevant services can be provided to the elderly, children, disabled people and other special groups to ensure their safety. It mainly include electronic fence, emergency call rescue, and other applications. Among them, the electronic fence can realize the setting of the scope of the electronic enclosure to prevent the relative people from going beyond that scope. Once they step out of that scope, the people who set up those scopes will receive alerts through their mobile phones immediately.

  • Mass Market Applications

    Mobile phones, wearable devices and other BDS public applications are gradually becoming new highlights in recent years. By using the BDS positioning function, the mobile navigation, route planning and a series of location service functions can be implement, which provide convenience to people's life. The sector mainly includes mobile phone applications, vehicle navigation equipment, wearable equipment, and other applications. Through the deep integration with information communication, internet of things, cloud computing and other technologies, many location service functions have been realized.

  • Electric Power Industry

    Time synchronization for electric power transmission involves national economy and the security of people's livelihood. Therefore, it is imperative to popularize BDS applications in electric power industry. By using the BDS timing function, the power management department can realize the unity of the time reference for the whole power network and ensure the safe and stable operation of the power grid. The sector mainly includes the applications of power grid time datum unification, the monitoring of the power station environment, the monitoring of power vehicles, and etc Among them, the high-precision BDS services is urgently needed for power grid time datum unification.

  • Finance

    The financial industry computer network time synchronization involves national politics, economy, people's livelihood and safety. The financial management department realizes the financial computer network time datum unification by using the BDS timing service function, and to ensure the safe and stable operation of the financial system. The sector mainly includes the applications of financial computer network time datum unification, financial vehicle supervision, and etc.
